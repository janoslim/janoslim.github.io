About

Hi! I’m Chaemin Lim, a Ph.D. student at Yonsei University / HPCP Lab, advised by Prof. Youngsok Kim.

I work on databases close to the metal: query engines, join algorithms, and execution strategies for GPUs and processing-in-memory (PIM) hardware. I care about making analytical workloads stop being bottlenecked by data movement.

Research Interests

  • Database acceleration on modern hardware (GPU, PIM, NVMe)
  • Query processing and engine architecture
  • Join algorithms and skew handling
  • Hardware-aware optimizer and compiler support
  • Concurrent and lock-free data structures
